## 📊 DEMOGRAPHIC OVERVIEW

**Primary Audience:**
- **Christian women** (25-55) seeking spiritual growth
- Women interested in prayer disciplines and deeper relationship with God
- Those exploring "spiritual warfare" and breakthrough prayer concepts
- Young adults (18-30) looking for faith-based lifestyle content
- Church small group leaders and women's ministry leaders

**What They're Looking For:**
- How to fast "the biblical way" (vs. secular dieting)
- Spiritual breakthrough and personal transformation
- Freedom from strongholds/bondages
- Fasting with prayer as a pathway to hearing God's voice
- Practical guides (21-day, 40-day fasts)
- Spiritual depth beyond surface-level faith

- **Printables & trackers convert well** — Great lead magnets
- **Fast + Pray is the common phrase** — Use in titles/copy
